Corporate matching is a great way to have your employer match your donations to MNS. Whether you are contributing to the [Annual Appeal]({{ '/annual-appeal/' | relative_url }}) or our auction, this program can add much-needed and appreciated support from the corporate community. Many corporations have a match program in place — simply ask your Human Resources department.

### How do I participate in the Matching Gifts program?

Follow these simple steps to **double your donation** at no cost to you:

1. Make a [donation]({{ site.external.generic_donate_url }}) to the MNS PTA — thank you!
2. We'll give you a receipt for your tax-deductible donation.
3. Ask your employer's Human Resources department if your company participates in a "matching gifts" program.
4. If yes, send the paperwork to us and we'll do the rest.
5. Any questions? Please contact [matchinggifts@mnspta.org](mailto:matchinggifts@mnspta.org) or [annualappeal@mnspta.org](mailto:annualappeal@mnspta.org).
