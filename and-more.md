The MNS PTA relies on you, the families, to keep our children's community thriving. Whether you like to be in the middle of all the action, prefer to work behind the scenes, want to head a committee for the year, or put in only an hour or two on a special task — there is a job for you. Any help is appreciated.

Have fun, get involved, and let's work together to keep our school a success.

For questions or to jump right in, drop us a line at [info@mnspta.org](mailto:info@mnspta.org).
