## Instructions for Neon sign-up

The Neon system helps families. You can sign up for events in advance, donate to the Annual Appeal, and make other school-related purchases. Neon also handles our email system and provides a Member Directory for MNS families. Since Neon is used for sending out email blasts from the PTA, **make sure your contact information is correct and your child's grade/class is updated for the current school year.**

### Don't know your Neon login information?

If you were an MNS family last year and have been receiving emails from the PTA, you already have a Neon account. If you are not sure of your login information, click [Log in now]({{ site.external.neon_login_url }}), enter your email address, then click the "Forgot Password" link. You'll be able to reset your password and access your account.

If you are a new MNS family, click [Log in now]({{ site.external.neon_login_url }}), then click "Create a new account." Please make sure to fill in the information for all your children at MNS so that you receive communication specific to your child's grade.

To be added to our Member Directory, you'll need to create a Membership: once you have created your account and logged in to Neon, click on **My Membership**. You may choose *One year term (automatically renew)* or *Life Join* — they are basically equivalent. Then click **submit**, and your contact information will be included in our Membership Directory.

### How do I access the Member Directory?

The Member Directory is available on the right-side menu once logged into Neon. If you would like to opt out of the Directory, you may do so by clicking on **Member Directory** and then **Update my profile listed on the membership directory**.
